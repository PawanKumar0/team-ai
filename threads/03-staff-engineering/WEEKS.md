# Staff Engineering Weeks

This file tells you what to write and prove each week in the staff engineering thread.

## Week 1

- artifacts:
  - product brief
  - architecture v1
- questions to answer:
  - what is the actual wedge?
  - what are the non-goals?
  - what must stay simple?

## Week 2

- artifact:
  - ADR for provider abstraction and request flow
- questions to answer:
  - what boundary is worth standardizing now?
  - what should stay easy to swap later?

## Week 3

- artifacts:
  - retrieval design review
  - ADR for retrieval and indexing strategy
- questions to answer:
  - what is source of truth?
  - what belongs in the vector store?

## Week 4

- artifact:
  - memory architecture note
- questions to answer:
  - what gets remembered?
  - what must never become implicit magic?

## Week 5

- artifact:
  - RFC on multi-agent justification
- questions to answer:
  - why is a workflow not enough?
  - what complexity is being bought?

## Week 6

- artifacts:
  - risk register
  - operational review
- questions to answer:
  - where does the system fail?
  - what must be monitored before trusting it?

## Week 7

- artifacts:
  - launch checklist
  - go/no-go review
- questions to answer:
  - what would block launch?
  - what is still too risky?

## Week 8

- artifacts:
  - postmortem
  - portfolio case study
  - promotion-style evidence summary
- questions to answer:
  - what did this project prove?
  - what should a hiring manager or promotion committee believe now?
