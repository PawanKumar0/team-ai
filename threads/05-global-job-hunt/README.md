## Goal

Turn Team AI into a globally marketable AI-engineering portfolio and use it to target roles outside India.

## Target Role Buckets

- AI engineer
- backend or platform engineer working on AI products
- applied AI engineer
- AI infra or tooling engineer
- forward-deployed engineer for AI products

## Geography Strategy

Use three lanes:

- remote-first global roles
- companies that explicitly sponsor relocation or work visas
- companies with regional hubs in UK, EU, Singapore, or UAE

Do not rely on old blog posts for immigration or sponsorship details.
Always verify directly on employer pages and official government sites because policies change.

## What You Need Before Applying

- one strong project case study
- one architecture deck
- one cleaned-up GitHub repo
- one resume tailored to AI engineering
- one short narrative for why you are credible in AI now
- one spreadsheet of target companies and contacts

## Weekly Job Hunt Loop

When activated, do this each week:

1. add 10 to 15 target companies
2. tailor resume and outreach for 3 to 5 roles
3. publish or refine one public proof artifact
4. do 2 mock interviews from the AI interview bank
5. follow up on referrals and applications

## Resume Narrative

Your story should be:

- strong engineer first
- now building AI systems with real architecture depth
- knows RAG, evals, orchestration, safety, and production trade-offs
- can write and reason like a staff-minded engineer

## Avoid

- applying broadly before the project is presentable
- sending the same resume everywhere
- claiming skills you cannot defend in design interviews
- optimizing for vanity companies instead of role fit
