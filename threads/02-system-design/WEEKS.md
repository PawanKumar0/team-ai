# System Design Weeks

This file tells you what to practice in the system design thread each week.

## Week 1

- general drill:
  - API gateway or URL shortener
- AI drill:
  - simple LLM assistant platform
- concepts:
  - request lifecycle
  - validation
  - idempotency
  - sync request flow

## Week 2

- general drill:
  - reliable webhook dispatcher
- AI drill:
  - model routing layer
- concepts:
  - retries
  - backoff
  - rate limits
  - fallbacks

## Week 3

- general drill:
  - job scheduler or notification service
- AI drill:
  - RAG ingestion and retrieval platform
- concepts:
  - async jobs
  - storage boundaries
  - indexing
  - freshness

## Week 4

- general drill:
  - auth service or cache service
- AI drill:
  - memory and session architecture
- concepts:
  - state recovery
  - concurrency
  - ownership
  - retention

## Week 5

- general drill:
  - pub-sub or config manager
- AI drill:
  - orchestration layer and agent/tool contracts
- concepts:
  - queues
  - checkpoints
  - orchestration vs choreography
  - remote interfaces

## Week 6

- general drill:
  - observability platform or rate limiter
- AI drill:
  - evaluation and tracing platform
- concepts:
  - SLOs
  - dashboards
  - failure taxonomy
  - online vs offline quality signals

## Week 7

- general drill:
  - billing flow or webhook consistency
- AI drill:
  - secure agent execution platform
- concepts:
  - authn/authz
  - tenancy
  - webhooks
  - sandboxing

## Week 8

- general drill:
  - choose one from auction, cab booking, trending topics, or notification system
- AI drill:
  - full enterprise AI copilot platform
- concepts:
  - system storytelling
  - trade-off communication
  - failure modes
  - phased rollout
