# Journal System

You said you want to document what you learn and what you build.

This folder is how you do that without turning documentation into overhead.

## Use These Templates

- Daily log: [templates/daily-log.md](/Users/pawan/Vizmo/team-ai/journal/templates/daily-log.md)
- Weekly review: [templates/weekly-review.md](/Users/pawan/Vizmo/team-ai/journal/templates/weekly-review.md)
- Design review: [templates/design-review.md](/Users/pawan/Vizmo/team-ai/journal/templates/design-review.md)
- Experiment card: [templates/experiment-card.md](/Users/pawan/Vizmo/team-ai/journal/templates/experiment-card.md)

## Recommended Rhythm

- one daily log per work session
- one weekly review every Friday or Sunday
- one design review whenever a decision is expensive or hard to reverse
- one experiment card for each meaningful prompt, model, retrieval, or orchestration change

## Naming

Keep naming simple:

- `YYYY-MM-DD-daily.md`
- `YYYY-week-N-review.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD-adr-or-design.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD-experiment.md`

## Why This Matters

This documentation becomes:

- your portfolio proof
- your interview evidence
- your postmortem memory
- your future product docs
- your staff-engineering signal
